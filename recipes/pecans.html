Mom's Pecans

    One bag of Costco Pecans (2lbs)

  1. Wash twice boil drain
  2. Melt 1/2 cup of brown sugar and some salt in water
  3. pecans in water and coat until water evaporates
  4. single layer on baking tray & parchment paper
  5. 330F for 20-22min for shallow rectangle tray 22-24min for thicker square tray
